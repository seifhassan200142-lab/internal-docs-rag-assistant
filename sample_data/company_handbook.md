# Company Handbook Sample

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Team leads may require in-office attendance for onboarding, critical planning sessions, or client workshops.

## Security Policy
Internal documents must not be uploaded to public AI tools. Confidential information should remain inside approved company systems. API keys and passwords must never be committed to source control.

## Expense Policy
Business travel expenses must be submitted within 14 days of the trip. Receipts are required for hotel, flight, and meal reimbursement.

## Support Policy
Employees should contact the IT help desk for laptop issues, account access problems, and approved software installation requests.
